# BrokeTheCycle

**Get through the next 12 minutes.**

BrokeTheCycle is a web-based application that helps people overcome addiction — not by counting streaks, but by getting you through the moment that matters: the craving. Native Android and iOS apps are on the roadmap.

---

## The Problem

Most recovery apps are streak counters with a journal bolted on. They turn recovery into a game where one slip = total failure. Users download in a moment of crisis, log 4 days, relapse, feel ashamed of the broken streak, and delete the app. The streak *is* the problem.

**BrokeTheCycle takes a different approach.** A craving peaks and fades in 10–20 minutes. The entire app is optimized for surviving that window — not tracking the months around it.

## How It Works

### The Craving Button

One big button on the home screen: **"I'm craving right now."**

Tap it and you enter a guided 12-minute protocol:

1. **Name it** (30 sec) — Identify the trigger. Even if you slip later, the data is captured.
2. **Breathe** (3 min) — Animated box breathing. 4-in, 4-hold, 4-out, 4-hold.
3. **Move** (5 min) — A walk timer. Get out of the environment.
4. **Reflect** (3 min) — Rate what changed. Log what helped.

No meditation fluff. No motivational quotes. Just a structured protocol to get you to the other side.

### Resilience Score (Not a Streak)

Streaks punish honesty. BrokeTheCycle uses a **Resilience Score** — a composite of cravings survived, sober days, and engagement. A relapse dips the score but never resets it. You climb back. This single design choice is the difference between a 3-week app and a 3-year app.

| Score | Label |
|-------|-------|
| 0–99 | Building |
| 100–299 | Steady |
| 300–599 | Strong |
| 600+ | Resilient |

### Craving Log

Every craving is data. The log shows patterns over time — which triggers repeat, what time of day hits hardest, which coping steps actually work for *you*.

## Design Principles

- **Zero friction at crisis.** Tap icon → tap button → start protocol. No login, no account, no email.
- **Privacy by default.** All data stored locally in your browser. No backend, no analytics SDK, no third-party tracking. Your recovery data never leaves your device.
- **No shame, ever.** The relapse flow says "This is part of recovery" — not "You failed." The app is designed so people come back after a slip instead of disappearing.
- **Direct, warm, never preachy.** Think: a thoughtful friend who knows recovery. Not a wellness app, not a clinician. No "journey." No "warrior." No "champion."

## Why "BrokeTheCycle"?

Addiction is a loop — trigger, craving, use, shame, repeat. Recovery isn't about willpower. It's about breaking the cycle, one craving at a time. The name is the proof.
